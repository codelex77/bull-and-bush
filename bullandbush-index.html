Our Story

Gaborone's Most
Legendary Pub

Since 1979, Bull & Bush has been the heartbeat of Gaborone's social scene. Nestled in Broadhurst, we've welcomed generations of locals and expatriates through our doors — and we're not stopping anytime soon.

We're famous for our fire-grilled steaks, wood-fired pizzas, and an ice-cold beer on a hot Botswana afternoon. Whether it's a quiet weeknight dinner, a rowdy sports screening, or live entertainment on the weekend — this is the place.
